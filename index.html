ABOUT ME

I love photography

Photography has always been a way for me to observe the world with curiosity and emotion. I enjoy capturing scenes that reflect everyday life, natural beauty, and the stories hidden in simple moments. Through my work, I aim to create images that feel honest, calm, and connected to reality.

My Name

Welcome to my website. My name is Mohammad Husaini, a freelance photographer born in Kabul, Afghanistan. I began my journey in photography at the age of 15, starting with a simple mobile phone. My experience spans social documentary, nature, macro, and street photography. Additionally, I have professional experience in the cinema industry as an assistant director and have worked as a behind-the-scenes photographer for various short and feature films, as well as cinematic projects in Afghanistan.
